Sludge production: iSBR 4,487 kg/d (Beca Table 6 PX,TSS median) → $491k/yr at $300/dry t; MBR derived from Beca alum component (Sec 6.7.3) + biological at industry-typical 0.87 SRT factor → 4,129 kg/d → $452k/yr. iSBR energy: 7,579 kWh/d derived from Beca Table 8 equipment (2 duty blowers @ 88 kW + 12 HCMAs @ 22 kW × 12 hr/day) + ancillaries placeholder. MBR energy creep resets at each membrane replacement event (new membranes = no fouling history). HNO3 and Mg(OH)2 doses remain placeholders pending Fonterra alkalinity data.
Electricity costed on energy basis (c/kWh) only. Industrial tariffs typically include demand and capacity components; for a sub-load on Fonterra's existing high-load site connection these are not expected to materially differentiate iSBR from MBR (both options run continuous baseload, marginal difference ~83 kW).
